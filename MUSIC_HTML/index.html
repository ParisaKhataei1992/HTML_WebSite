Music In Our Life

Studies reveal that music can affect your life and your mood. Music is embedded in people’s daily life either at work or when you are relaxing at home. Each person’ brain reacts differently to music. Some people do not listen to the music which makes everyone to ask this question: "How Could You Live without Music?”. There are many types of music genre that some of which are very popular among young people such as pop, hip hop and country music and make them feel more energetic for doing their activities.
