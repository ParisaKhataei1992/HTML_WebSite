How Music Promotes Our Health

Music has an important role in body health. It is like a medicine for the body and soul. Have you ever know that music can reduce pain especially chronic pain? Nowadays, doctors advise that playing music during child birth in hospitals decreases use of medication in order to pain control. There is something more about music and its effect on health. Listening to the music can reduce blood pressure. Researchers have illustrated by monitoring some young people when they are listening to the different genre of music. They realized that when a person with high blood pressure is listening to a relaxation and meditation music, he or she can control and lower his or her blood pressure. Scientists have shown that classical music especially Beethoven symphonies is very helpful for the brain of newborn babies. They believe if you play this type of music for babies under 2 years old, it really benefits their health in terms of learning skill improvement and even physical health. Study showed that listening to Mozart K 448 (Sonata for Two Pianos in D major) helps children suffering from epilepsy.
